About

This portfolio showcases my work for the "Art of the Web" course, exploring digital media, web design principles, and interactive experiences. The website design is inspired by the aesthetic of House of Gul, featuring a minimalist approach with careful attention to typography and layout.

Through this course, I've developed skills in HTML, CSS, and p5.js while considering user interaction and experience design principles.
