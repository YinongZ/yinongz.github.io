About Throne Entertainment

Our Story

Welcome to Throne Entertainment, where we celebrate and elevate the universal human experience of... well, sitting on the toilet.

Founded in 2025 by a group of creative minds with too much time in the bathroom, we asked ourselves: "Why should toilet time be wasted time?" We realized that those precious minutes (or sometimes longer) could be transformed into moments of learning, laughter, or reflection.

Our mission is simple: provide bite-sized, engaging content perfectly timed for your bathroom breaks. From fascinating trivia and quick games to thought-provoking micro-essays and mini-meditations, we've got your throne time covered.
