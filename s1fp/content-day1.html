Bathroom Trivia

Did You Know?

Welcome to today's bathroom break entertainment! Here are some fascinating toilet trivia facts to ponder:

Ancient Roman Toilets

In ancient Rome, public toilets were social spaces where people would chat and even conduct business meetings while using facilities that often had 20 or more seats in a single room!

The Average Time

The average person spends about 1.5 years of their life in the bathroom. That's a lot of potential reading time!

World Toilet Day

November 19th is officially recognized as World Toilet Day by the United Nations to raise awareness about the global sanitation crisis.

Thomas Crapper

Contrary to popular belief, Thomas Crapper did not invent the flush toilet, though he did improve its design and popularized indoor plumbing in the 19th century.

Space Toilets

Toilets on the International Space Station cost about $19 million each and use suction to compensate for the lack of gravity.
