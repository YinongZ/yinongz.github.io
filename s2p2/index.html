About

My work explores the interplay between architecture, collective memory, and public perception, emphasizing contextual responses that integrate local narratives and environmental considerations to foster meaningful interactions between people and their surroundings.

I am particularly interested in using computational tools to translate cultural references into contemporary architectural forms, aiming to reshape how we experience and engage with built environments.

With a background spanning both traditional architectural methodologies and emerging digital fabrication techniques, I approach each project as an opportunity to balance innovative solutions with timeless design principles.
